01 — Overview

The Event

What we produced, what it generated, and why it matters.

What Happened

Kozo. 1965 Mustang. 500+ hours. One room that mattered.

On May 14, 2026, No. 9 Agency co-produced the VIP dinner and press preview for Kozo's "Permanent Impermanence" — a solo exhibition presented by CART Department at Free Parking, a 19th-century West Village carriage house at 16 Morton Street. The centerpiece: a 1965 Ford Mustang Coupe covered in 500+ hours of hand-painted and tattooed art, including a full reinterpretation of François Lemoyne's Apotheosis of Hercules from the Palace of Versailles.

The exhibition ran May 15–24. The dinner and VIP preview on May 14 set the cultural context for everything that followed — press coverage, brand partnerships, and relationships now extending into future programming.

The Dinner

Chef Rōze Traore. HexClad. A menu built for the room.

Chef Rōze Traore (@rozetraore) curated a multi-course menu in dialogue with the exhibition: Yellowfin Crudo with pickled daikon and nori dust / Branzino with saffron beurre blanc and black truffles / Poached pears with hibiscus and goat cheese. Chef Roze accepted a below-market fee to participate. That is a direct result of the relationship No. 9 Agency built and the caliber of room we created. HexClad — a direct No. 9 network relationship — co-presented the dinner alongside Kozo. The dinner in its form doesn't exist without us.

Follower Trajectory

Venice purge → event recovery → 1,000,129.

Going into the Venice Biennale trip (May 6–10), Kozo was at approximately 1M followers. Instagram's bot purge during that window dropped him to the low 999K range — nearly 1,000 followers lost in days. The event week, followed by three editorial placements in 72 hours, drove the full recovery back above 1M.

The 30-day view chart shows a clear two-wave pattern: a sustained pre-event spike starting around May 3, peaking at approximately 395K views in a single day as anticipation content built momentum, then a second acceleration during the press window — Cool Hunting May 17, Whitewall May 19, Surface May 20 — pushing the account back past the milestone. Views during the event 14-day window alone: 1,720,996. That is the direct output of the room No. 9 built on May 14.
