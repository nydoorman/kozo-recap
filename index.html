01 — Overview

The Event

What we produced, what it generated, and why it matters.

What Happened

Kozo. 1965 Mustang. 500+ hours. One room that mattered.

On May 14, 2026, No. 9 Agency co-produced the VIP dinner and press preview for Kozo's "Permanent Impermanence" — a solo exhibition presented by CART Department at Free Parking, a 19th-century carriage house at 16 Morton Street, West Village. The centerpiece: a 1965 Ford Mustang Coupe covered in 500+ hours of hand-painted art, including a full reinterpretation of François Lemoyne's Apotheosis of Hercules from the Palace of Versailles.

The exhibition ran May 15–24, 2026. The dinner and VIP preview on May 14 set the cultural context for everything that followed. Press coverage hit within 72 hours. Brand relationships built through No. 9 are now extending into future programming.

The Venue

Free Parking, 16 Morton Street

CART Department's West Village gallery — a 19th-century carriage house with a lineage that includes exhibitions featuring Keith Haring and Ai Weiwei. Kozo's presentation is described by Whitewall as "one of the platform's most immersive exhibitions to date."

"One monumental 1965 Ford Mustang, adorned with more than 500 hours' worth of hand-painted and tattooed art, greets visitors. The exhibition edifies, inspires, and builds synapses between art forms."

Surface Magazine — May 20, 2026

The Dinner

Chef Rōze Traore. HexClad. A menu that matched the work.

Chef Rōze Traore (@rozetraore) — known for blending food with storytelling — curated a multi-course menu in dialogue with the exhibition: Yellowfin Crudo with pickled daikon and nori dust, Branzino with saffron beurre blanc and black truffles, poached pears with hibiscus and goat cheese. HexClad cookware appeared in active service throughout, becoming part of the event's visual language.

Chef Roze accepted a below-market fee to participate in this project. That's a direct result of the relationship No. 9 Agency built and the quality of the room we created. HexClad — the dinner's primary co-presenter — is a direct No. 9 network connection. That dinner doesn't exist in this form without us.
